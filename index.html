About Me



I am currently a Junior at NC State. Growing up, I was a jack of all trades. My curiosity always bounced me around many different hobbies and interests. When I discovered the outlet that coding could be for my creativity, I was hooked. Since then I've gone through coding camps over the summers of my childhood, and pursued my passion at NCSU. I've worked as an analyst for Disney Streaming and my experiences there further boosted my drive. I love being able to incorporate other interests I've accumulated into this part of my life. I hope to continue to surprise myself with the limitlessness of Computer Science.

Projects



Sentiment Analysis Bot


Intended for a wide variety of businesses including media/entertainment companies, social media managers, and customer support teams. The bot analyzes text and can connect to the Reddit API to analyze and graph the most prominent emotions present.



AES Encrypter and Decrypter


Intended for companies in need of safely storing data encrypted with AES encryption. This program will allow users to encrypt or decrypt their own files using an RSA key pair. The program can encrypt and decrypt messages of 16-bit length.
